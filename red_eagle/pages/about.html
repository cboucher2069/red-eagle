FAQ

1How to send an image for custom clothing?

You will be able to upload your desire image to a piece of clothing when you select the item and press custom.

2 How long will shipping take?

Depending on how far you live shipping should take about 2-4 days

3What can you not get printed on a shirt?

You aren't allowed to upload any copyrighted images such as Nike swoosh or an Adidas logo.

4 How long will it take for the image to be printed on the shirt?

Depending on how big the image is it should take a day or two and then will be shipped the following day.

5Can I get a custom image on anything?

Yes, you will be able to print an image on any piece of clothing or accessory that we have available.

6How many custom shirts can I purchase at once?

You will be able to purchase up to 20 shirts at a time and mass order on particular images.

7Can you also purchase shirts for children and infants?

Yes, we have a wide range of clothing sizes ranging from adults to infants.
